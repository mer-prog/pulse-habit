### Full Description (EN)

Build better habits, one day at a time.

PulseHabit is a beautifully designed habit tracker that helps you build and maintain positive daily routines. With its distinctive Neo-Brutalist design, tracking your habits has never felt this satisfying.

**Core Features:**
• Create unlimited habits with custom icons and colors
• Track daily completions with a single tap
• View your streaks and stay motivated
• Beautiful statistics with weekly and monthly views
• Dark mode support (Light / Dark / System)
• Cloud sync across devices
• Offline-first — works without internet
• Customizable reminders

**Your Data, Your Control:**
• Data stored locally on your device first
• Optional cloud sync with end-to-end security
• No ads, no tracking, no data selling
• Export your data anytime
